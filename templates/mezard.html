Scoracle

Scoracle is an AI powered predictor

Scoracle, a sports data analysis platform, is built using a Python-based tech stack. One of the key features of Scoracle is predicting football match outcomes. We utilize the Poisson formula, implemented with the SciPy module, to model the probability of goals scored by each team in a match. This mathematical model takes into account various factors such as team strength, home or away status, and past performance. The prediction algorithm processes this data, applies the Poisson formula, and computes the probability of different match outcomes like win, loss, or draw. These predictions are then presented to users via the frontend. This architecture showcases our expertise in statistical modeling, algorithm development, and web technologies, making Scoracle an excellent fit for roles that emphasize sports analytics, statistical modeling, and web development.

Tools used:Python, Django, Render, Bootstrap, SciPy, Beautiful Soup
